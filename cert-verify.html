Certificate Format

Certificate Number Format

How Certificate Numbers Are Structured
AISUCERT[ProgramCode][Year][SerialNo]
Example: AISUCERTCOMP2026000145
AISU
Organization Prefix – All India Students Union
CERT
Certificate Identifier – denotes this is a certificate
COMP
Program Code – Competition / Internship / Innovation etc.
2026
Year – Year when the certificate was issued
000145
Serial Number – Unique incremental number per certificate
Every certificate issued by AISU carries a unique certificate number. No two certificates share the same number, ensuring secure and reliable verification.
