Grievance Redressal

File a Complaint

AISU's Complaint Portal provides a secure and confidential mechanism for students to file grievances. After logging in, you can file a complaint and your personal details remain strictly confidential throughout the entire process.

How the Process Works
1
Login & File Complaint

Student logs in and submits their complaint. Identity is protected – only the complaint ID is visible to sub-national teams.

2
Complaint Reviewed

National team has full visibility. State/District/Mandal teams access only via the unique Complaint ID – no personal details visible.

3
Actions Recorded & Communicated

Each concerned team records action taken. Updates are automatically emailed to the complainant at each step.

4
Complaint Disposed

Once resolved, the complaint is formally disposed with complete documentation and final resolution email to complainant.

Who Can See What
🏛️ National Team
Full complaint visibility – all details including student information
🗺️ State / District / Mandal / College Teams
Can view complaint details ONLY using the unique Complaint ID – no student personal details visible
