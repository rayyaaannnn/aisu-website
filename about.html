Who We Are

All India Students Union

All India Students Union (AISU) is a national-level registered body dedicated to student empowerment, leadership development, talent identification, and transparent grievance redressal. An initiative of the Federation of Indian Youth Association, we are committed to representing the interests and concerns of students nationwide.

We believe in the power of students to shape the future of our country through education and leadership. Our structured hierarchy operates from national level down to state, district, mandal, and institutional levels, ensuring our reach is truly national and inclusive.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

—

Our Vision

To empower students and individuals through leadership, education, competitions, ethical governance, and structured grievance redressal at national, state, district, and local levels.

We envision a generation of confident, engaged, and socially responsible individuals who actively contribute to the progress of India.

Our Mission

  • To create equal opportunities for students and professionals
  • To identify and nurture talent through competitions
  • To provide internships and organizational affiliations
  • To ensure transparency and accountability through structured complaint handling
  • To build a strong organizational hierarchy with measurable performance tracking
  • To advocate for quality education reform in India

Internal Structure

Departments & Special Cells

AISU operates through specialized departments and cells, each responsible for a specific functional domain within the organization.

Legal Cell

Handles legal compliance, drafts MOU documents, oversees complaint resolution legal framework, and ensures the organization operates within applicable laws. Provides legal awareness to student members.

IT Cell

Manages the organization's technology infrastructure including the website, member portal, competition platform, certificate generation system, and all digital tools. Drives AISU's digital transformation.

Social Media Cell

Manages AISU's presence across Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, YouTube, and LinkedIn. Creates campaigns, handles content strategy, and amplifies student voices on national platforms. Coordinates promotional activities.

Press Department

Responsible for issuing press releases, managing media relationships, publishing organizational news and updates, and coordinating with journalists covering student affairs and youth empowerment stories nationwide.

Innovation Cell (ICell)

Promotes student innovation, entrepreneurship, and research. Students can submit innovation proposals for evaluation. Accepted proposals receive mentoring support, prototype funding guidance, and partnership with NewTapWorld.in for incubation.

EmpowHer Wing

Dedicated to women empowerment within the student community. Organizes women-centric competitions, leadership workshops, and mentoring programs. Ensures equal participation and addresses gender-specific issues in academic institutions.

Anti-Ragging Wing

Actively works to prevent and address ragging incidents across affiliated institutions. Coordinates with administrators, processes anti-ragging complaints through the confidential complaint portal, and runs awareness campaigns in colleges.
