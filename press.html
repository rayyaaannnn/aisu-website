Press Statement

Promotion of Ms. M. Indraja as National Organizing Secretary

04 May 2026

Official Press Statement

The All India Students Union (AISU) is pleased to formally announce the promotion of Miss. M. Indraja to the esteemed position of National Organizing Secretary, in recognition of her unwavering dedication, exceptional administrative capabilities, and consistent contributions to organizational growth and excellence. AISU also extends its heartfelt congratulations to Miss. Indraja on the occasion of her promotion and upcoming completion of three years of distinguished service on 22 July 2026, a tenure marked by an impeccable record without a single remark or dispute. Her journey stands as a model of commitment, discipline, and leadership.

Miss. Indraja began her organizational journey as District General Secretary, Srikakulam District, Andhra Pradesh, on 22 July 2023. Through her persistent efforts and remarkable performance, she was successively elevated to the positions of State Additional Organizing Secretary (Administration) and State Organizing Secretary, before attaining her current national-level responsibility. Throughout her tenure, she has played a crucial and proactive role in various key functional bodies of AISU including Anti-Ragging Squads, Executive Committees, and multiple Competition Organizing Committees. Her active involvement in decision-making processes, meticulous coordination of programs, and ability to execute large-scale initiatives have significantly strengthened the organizations operational framework. Her contributions in organizing academic, cultural, and leadership-oriented competitions and programs have been widely acknowledged, reflecting her strong organizational acumen and visionary approach.

Statements from Office Bearers:
Mr. Digamber Kumar Dubey, Chairman, FIYA & National President, AISU stated: "Miss. Indrajas journey over the past three years exemplifies integrity, commitment, and disciplined leadership. She has consistently demonstrated the ability to handle responsibilities with maturity and precision, contributing meaningfully to organizational development. Her proactive involvement in planning, execution, and team coordination makes her highly deserving of this national-level responsibility."

Mr. M. Sai Krishna, National Vice-President, AISU stated: "Over the past three years, Miss. Indraja has demonstrated exceptional administrative excellence, strategic vision, and an unwavering commitment to AISUs core values. Her active participation in policy formulation, structured execution of national and state programs, and leadership in key committees such as anti-ragging squads and executive bodies have significantly strengthened institutional mechanisms. She has shown remarkable capability in coordinating large teams, ensuring transparency, and maintaining accountability in operations. Her elevation to National Organizing Secretary is a well-deserved recognition of her consistent performance, leadership qualities, and dedication to strengthening AISU at the national level."

Mr. Navneet Singh, National General Secretary, AISU stated: "Miss. Indraja has been a pillar of strength for AISU through her consistency, sincerity, and strong work ethic. Her ability to efficiently manage multiple responsibilities while maintaining high standards of execution has greatly contributed to organizational progress. Her promotion reflects both her merit and the confidence she has earned through her sustained contributions."

Miss. Sk. Sameera, National Joint Secretary, AISU stated: "The dedication and tireless service demonstrated by Miss. Indraja continue to inspire many within AISU. Her commitment to teamwork, disciplined execution, and organizational values has set a benchmark for emerging leaders. This promotion is a rightful recognition of her continuous efforts and the positive impact she has created within the organization."

The All India Students Union (AISU) expresses its deep appreciation for Miss. Indrajas tireless efforts and looks forward to her continued contributions at the national level. Her journey exemplifies leadership through service, and her elevation is a testament to merit, integrity, and hard work.

Issued by: Miss. Tisha Tadav, National General Secretary, Social Media & Press Departments, All India Students Union (AISU).
