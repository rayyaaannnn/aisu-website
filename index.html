About Us

Empowering Students Across India

All India Students Union (AISU) is a national-level registered body committed to student empowerment, leadership development, and transparent governance. An initiative of the Federation of Indian Youth Association, we operate from national down to mandal level across the country.

"Education is the most powerful weapon we can use to change the world."

— Nelson Mandela
  • Leadership development and structured opportunities
  • Transparent grievance redressal at all levels
  • National talent identification through competitions

What We Offer

Our Key Initiatives

🏅

Primary Membership

Join as an official member and receive a unique AISU ID, login credentials, appointment letter, and access to organizational dashboards.
🎓

Student Membership

Register as a student member and gain free access to all competitions for one year with a unique AISUSM identification number.
🏆

Competition Portal

Participate in essay, painting, poster, quiz, and video competitions. Winners receive auto-generated certificates with unique IDs.
🛡️

Complaint Portal

File confidential complaints through our secure grievance redressal system. Your identity remains fully protected throughout the process.
💼

Internship

Apply for internships facilitated by AISU. Track your application status from selection through completion within your login account.
🤝

Organization Affiliation

External organizations can apply to affiliate with AISU. Applications are reviewed exclusively by the national-level team.
Our Message

Arise • Awake • Empower

All India Students Union (AISU), an initiative of the Federation of Indian Youth Association, is dedicated to the betterment of the education system in India. As an organization run by students, teachers, and professors, AISU is committed to representing the interests and concerns of students nationwide.

Our core objective is to empower students and youth to actively participate in creating a better India. We provide a platform for you to voice your issues and concerns related to education, and we work tirelessly to bring these matters to the attention of concerned authorities, advocating for appropriate reforms.
