About ICell

Where Innovation Meets Opportunity

The AISU Innovation Cell (ICell) provides a dedicated platform for students, researchers, and innovators to submit their innovative ideas, research proposals, and project concepts. We bridge the gap between vision and implementation.

Approved proposals receive structured support including mentorship, development assistance, and access to investors. Funds received from investors are directly transferred to the applicant's account on their behalf.

Post Approval

What Happens After Approval?

Expert Mentorship

Approved innovators receive dedicated mentorship from experienced professionals and subject matter experts to refine and develop their ideas further.

Development Support

ICell provides technical and organizational support to help innovators build prototypes, MVPs, and implement their solutions effectively.

Investor Funding

ICell forwards proposals to investors on behalf of the applicant. Once funds are received from investors, they are deposited directly into the candidate's provided bank account.
