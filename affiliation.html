Affiliation

Affiliate with AISU

External organizations can apply for affiliation with AISU. When an affiliation application is submitted, an acknowledgment email is sent to the applicant, and the complete application details are forwarded exclusively to the national-level team for review and approval.

Affiliation Process
1
Submit Application
Organization representative fills and submits the affiliation form with complete details.
2
Acknowledgment Email
An acknowledgment confirmation email is automatically sent to the applying organization.
3
National Team Review
Application is forwarded exclusively to the National Team for centralized review and decision-making.
4
Approval & Status Update
Decision communicated to applicant and affiliation status managed through the Admin Panel.
Benefits of Affiliation
National recognition through AISU network
Collaborative opportunities in competitions and events
Access to student networks across India
Centralized and transparent decision-making process
